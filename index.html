About Me

I'm a graphic designer and web developer with over ten years' combined experience. My first job, before pursuing a career in graphic design, was working for my brother as a carpenter. He designs and manufactures award-winning furniture and I'm grateful for my formative years spent with him as I learned the values of exceptional workmanship.

Through hard work, passion and determination I became the leader of a world class printing department at Yesman. From there I dived into web development and came to join ZaiLab - an amazing tech startup set to revolutionise the call center industry.

When I'm not working hard to make the web more beautiful you can find me spending endless hours on the internet either reading about best practices, the latest trends or admiring awesome designs.

My favourite sites are: smashingmagazine.com | alistapart.com | thisiscolossal.com and sometimes just for fun: manbabies.com
