About Me

My name is James Wilson, I'm a graphic designer with one year's experience in the clothing industry and seven years' in the print industry. My first job before pursuing a career in graphic design was working for my brother in carpentry. He designs and manufactures award-winning furniture and I am grateful for my formative years spent with him as I learnt the value of exceptional workmanship. Throughout my career I have always gone the extra mile and it's through my efforts and hard work ethic that I earned the respect of my colleagues and became the leader of a world-class printing department at Yesman. I enjoy creating brands and find great reward in creating something that hits the mark for the client in more ways than one. I have a passion for all things creative, so when I'm not working hard to make the world more beautiful you can find me spending endless hours on the internet either reading or admiring awesome designs. My favourite sites are: smashingmagazine.com | visualnews.com | thisiscolossal.com and sometimes just for fun: manbabies.com
